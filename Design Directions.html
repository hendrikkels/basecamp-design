basecamp / design system / phase 01 — directions

Three ways to build the room.

The brief: a personal web app — landing, dashboard, widgets, blog — that reads as part terminal, part editorial document. Below are three coherent directions that share the same foundation (color tone, mono + heavy display pairing, hairline rules, near-mono palette with one pop) but differ in density, accent, and motif. Pick one and I’ll go deep: the full component library, layouts, and the linked mocks.
